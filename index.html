Ganggang Dong

I graduat from National University of Defense Technology in Dec., 2016, and join in Xidian University in Nov., 2018. My research interests include but are not limited to 1) the application of compressed sensing and sparse representation, 2) SAR image interpretation, e.g., target recognition, image segmentation, terrain classification, 3) machine learning, 4) pattern recognition.
