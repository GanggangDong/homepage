Biograph

I am a free-researcher currently. My research interests include but are not limited to 1) the application of compressed sensing and sparse representation, 2) SAR image interpretation, e.g., target recognition, image segmentation, terrain classification, SAR imaging, 3) machine learning, e.g., manifold learning, filter bank, nonlinear dimension reduction, data fusion, and low-rank representation, 4) pattern recognition.
